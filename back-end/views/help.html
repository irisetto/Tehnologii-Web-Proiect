Frequent questions

  • Where can I change the theme?
    From the menu above press SETTINGS and there you will find the theme option.
  • Where can I find my profile?
    In the menu above, top left, you can see a person icon. Press on that and there it is your profile.
  • How to sign out?
    In the menu above, top right, you can see a door with an arrow icon. Press on that and you will sign out.
  • How can I see just the existing birds in the zoo?
    First option: Press on the logo in the middle of the menu to access home. From there you can press directly on the birds category.
    Second option: Go to ANIMALS from the menu above and choose class -> birds from the filter section.
  • Where is the menu for the home page?
    In the menu above you can see the logo of our zoo in the middle, press on that and you will reach home.

You don’t find your problem here? Fill in the form and we’ll reach out to you!
